Analogue

Born and raised in Mauritania, I earned a merit-based government scholarship that launched an academic journey across three countries — from ranking 51st out of 4,000 on the Tunisian national exam to graduating 1st at Tunisia Polytechnic School, and now pursuing a PhD at KAUST's Clean Combustion Research Center in Saudi Arabia. Along the way, conferences have taken me from Singapore to Edinburgh to Vancouver and beyond. When I'm not in the lab working on laser spectroscopy or training machine learning models, these are the things that keep me curious, grounded, and human.
